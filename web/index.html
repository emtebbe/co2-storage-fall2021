Our Mission

Our mission is to enable rapid carbon sequestration projects using data science.

  • Match CO2 emitters to depleted oilfields to identify optimal storage projects using data science
  • Allow California EPA to find an affordable path toward net-zero emissions
  • Provide extra benefit of redeploying oilfield workers in the CCS industry

Our team of UC Berkeley data scientists is looking at carbon recapture, putting emissions back into the ground. It is essential to every net-zero climate strategy. But existing plans for state-wide pipelines are too slow.

California already has widespread depleted oil fields where you could sequester carbon safely.

We seek to support regulators to help them optimize this capture immediately, building on existing regulations.

We'd love your perspective on this, and to know what is possible in California. Please explore the scenarios on our dashboard to understand the tax credit incentives required for short- or long-term projects storing industrial emissions within depleted oil fields.
